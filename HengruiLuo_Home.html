I'm Hengrui Luo (Henry).
Currently, I am a Postdoc Researcher at the Computational Research Department, Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory at Berkeley.
I obtained my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Statistics, The Ohio State University at Columbus in 2020.
I obtained my B.Sc. degree from Department of Mathematics, Sun Yat-sen University at Guangzhou in 2015.

My current research interests include
    Bayesian Methods. e.g. Bayesian optimization, scalable Bayesian methods and Gaussian processes.
    Topological Data Analysis. e.g. Topology of random sets, dimension reduction and functional data analysis.
    High dimensional Probability. e.g. Sketching and randomized algorithms.
